Always Be Coding (ABC's of my life)

As an individual I always wanted to communicate with machine. In my initial days of Engineering, understanding machine language was difficult for me, but I never gave up. I had difficulty to know where to start and In my under graduate and post graduate studies I ended up with hardware learning and a high level knowledge of software engineering. I seriously started understanding 0's and 1's of computer coding.

I moved to San Antonio,Texas and started learning Data Mining and SQL stuff by myself. It gave me confidence and I felt comfortable talking to databases. After couple of years of traveling in US, I landed in one of the most amazing and eventual city NASHVILLE, TN. I joined Nashville Software School to learn and practice real time coding in various platforms starting from basic HTML,CSS,JS to NodeJS. I graduated as a Full Stack Developer from NSS in May 2017, at which time I was one of the catalyst in the class "for showing leadership, commitment, and dedication".If I can channel that passion into business solutions that make a positive difference in my community, all the better.
